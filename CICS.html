About CICS

College of Information and Computing Studies (CICS) stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the Iloilo region. Committed to holistic development, we strive to provide world-class education that empowers students to become global citizens and catalysts of change.

Our Vision: A globally competitive State University in Asia.

Our Mission:Human resources development through quality and relevant education, environment-friendly modern technologies and preservation of Filipino values and culture for sustainable and improved quality of life..
