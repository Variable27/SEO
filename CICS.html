About CICS

The College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS) at Northern Iloilo State University is a leader in technological education and innovation. CICS is renowned for its state-of-the-art programs in Computer Science, Information Systems, and Information Technology, equipping students with skills relevant to modern enterprise computing, including Customer Information Control System (CICS) technologies.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

A 4-year program emphasizing advanced computational theories and cutting-edge applications in artificial intelligence, software engineering, data science, and enterprise systems like IBM CICS.

Bachelor of Library in Information Sciences

Focuses on the design, development, and management of information systems, including Customer Information Control System (CICS) technologies to enhance business processes and decision-making..

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Develops practical expertise in network management, cloud computing, and software solutions for diverse industries.
